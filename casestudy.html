SQL Top 3 Students Query

Description

In this query we have to store the marks of students and calculate top 3 students.
Criteria: Student should have aggregate more than 60% and he should not be fail in any subject (i.e. marks < 30) and he should also not be absent in any subject.

Example

  • Exactly 4 students failing overall
  • 2 students marked absent in one subject each
  • One of the absentees having the highest aggregate
  • Aggregate and percentage columns included
